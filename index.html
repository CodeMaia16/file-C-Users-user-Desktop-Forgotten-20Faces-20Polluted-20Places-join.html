Shining light on the forgotten

ABOUT

Forgotten Faces, Polluted Places is built on one simple belief: Every person deserves to live in a place that allows them to breathe, learn, grow, and feel seen. Many communities live with pollution not because they don’t care, but because they have been overlooked for too long hidden in the shadows of society. This aims to bring light into these spaces. Not a spotlight Just a warm, steady radiance that reminds people they matter.

WHO ARE THE FORGOTTEN?

The Forgotten, When we speak of the forgotten, we’re not talking about people who don’t exist. We are talking about those whose voices are rarely heard, whose needs are often overlooked and whose struggles are hidden in the shadows of society. They are the children who cannot access clean classrooms, the families living amid polluted markets, the youth with ideas but no platform, and communities battling illness without proper awareness or care.
